Who Are We?

We are Lambda Alpha Nu, the co-ed social fraternity for students interested in computing and technology at the University of Texas at Austin. ΛΑΝ cultivates future startup founders, engineers at top tech companies, graduate students, teaching assistants, front-end developers, and most importantly, good people who are passionate about technology. We are focused on social activities and building community in a laid back, inclusive environment. We will be accepting applications from all students who are interested in tech.
